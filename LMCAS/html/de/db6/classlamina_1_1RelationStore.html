详细描述

Stores relational constraints and derives sign properties for simple patterns.

When a relation of the form variable op 0 is added, the RelationStore notifies the PropertyStore to mark the variable with the corresponding sign:

  • GT → Positive
  • GEQ → NonNegative
  • LT → Negative
  • LEQ → NonPositive
  • NEQ → NonZero

Composite relations (multi-variable LHS or non-zero RHS) are stored without decomposition for later use by the InferenceEngine.
