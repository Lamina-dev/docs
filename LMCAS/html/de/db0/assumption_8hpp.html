Core enumerations and forward declarations for the Assumption System.

Defines Domain, Sign, Parity, Boundedness, and Tribool enumerations used throughout the assumption-based reasoning layers (PropertyStore, RelationStore, InferenceEngine, AssumptionContext, QueryInterface).
